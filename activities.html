We offer a number of exciting activies for both adults and children.

  • Surf lessons for all ages takes place every Friday, Saturday and Sunday starting at 10am and 2pm on each of those days. All ages are welcome.
  • Banana Boats are fun for the whole family! We offer boat rides 7 days a week. The times offered are 10am, 12pm, 2pm and 4pm.
  • For the adults, we offer a wine tasting and Caribbean cooking class on Fridays, Saturdays and Sundays starting at 5pm.

Please see our front desk to schedule all activities.
